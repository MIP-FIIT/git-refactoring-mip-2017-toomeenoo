Lift stopwatch

Version: 1.0 - Early relase

This app will help you to stay in rythm during your exercise. Simply set up parameters how fast and how long you want to lift (does not matter what) and press start.

Only early relase, this app will have extended usability like sound signalization, customizations, statistics and planed trainings.

If you like this app and want it to be updated, rate it (well) on AppStore / Google Play / Windows store.
